Infor WMS vs. Infor M3 Factory Track

Infor WMS

An enterprise-grade warehouse management system designed to optimize every aspect of warehouse operations, from receiving to shipping.

Primary Focus:

  • Receiving & Putaway
  • Picking & Packing
  • Inventory Management
  • Load Management
  • Space Optimization
  • Labor Tracking
  • Shipping & Loading

Infor M3 Factory Track

A manufacturing execution system that extends the M3 ERP to the factory floor, providing real-time visibility into production activities, labor, and material consumption.

Primary Focus:

  • Shop Floor Execution
  • Material Consumption
  • Quality Management

The Power of System-Directed Workflows

RF Data Collection

A method for capturing data, it is **worker-led** and operates reactively, logging transactions after they occur.

Platforms:

  • Infor M3 Factory Track

Characteristics:

  • Worker decides their own path and task order.
  • System acts as a simple data logger.
  • Provides basic metrics on completed transactions.
  • Can be inefficient due to manual decision-making.

System-Directed

A core feature of a modern WMS, it is **system-led** and proactive, using advanced logic to orchestrate every task for efficiency.

Platforms:

  • Infor WMS

Characteristics:

  • WMS determines the most efficient path and next best task.
  • System uses rules to interleave and prioritize tasks.
  • Provides advanced metrics on productivity and travel time.
  • Eliminates guesswork and significantly boosts efficiency.
